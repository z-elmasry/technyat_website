# Technyat Website Design Approach

## Brand Identity (from PDF)
- **Company Name:** Technyat Solutions
- **Tagline:** Building Construction Futures
- **Primary Colors:**
  - Deep Black: #111111
  - Pure Black: #000000
  - Dark Gray: #3c3c3c
  - Medium Gray: #939598
  - Accent Orange: #dd5126
- **Typography:** Alexandria font (supports multiple weights: Thin, ExtraLight, Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, ExtraBold, Black)

## Design Philosophy: Modern Industrial Elegance

### Core Principles
1. **Sophisticated Minimalism** - Clean layouts with strategic use of whitespace, avoiding clutter while maintaining visual hierarchy
2. **Bold Contrast** - Deep blacks paired with the vibrant orange accent (#dd5126) to create visual impact and guide user attention
3. **Structural Clarity** - Geometric precision reflecting construction industry expertise, with intentional asymmetry to avoid generic corporate feel

### Color Philosophy
- **Background:** Pure white (#FFFFFF) with strategic dark sections (#111111, #000000) for dramatic contrast
- **Accent:** Orange (#dd5126) reserved for CTAs, highlights, and key visual elements to draw attention
- **Typography:** Dark grays (#3c3c3c, #939598) for readable body text with black for headlines
- **Emotional Intent:** Professional authority, innovation, and reliability combined with energy and forward momentum

### Layout Paradigm
- **Hero Section:** Full-width with diagonal/angled cuts using SVG dividers to create visual interest
- **Asymmetric Grid:** Alternating left-right content blocks with images on one side, text on the other
- **Floating Elements:** Strategic use of floating cards and offset sections to create depth
- **Sectional Breaks:** Orange accent dividers and geometric shapes between major sections

### Signature Elements
1. **Diagonal SVG Dividers** - Angular transitions between sections using orange accent
2. **Floating Card Blocks** - Services and features displayed in elevated cards with subtle shadows
3. **Geometric Accents** - Orange rectangles, circles, and lines strategically placed for visual rhythm

### Interaction Philosophy
- **Smooth Transitions:** Hover effects on buttons and cards with 300ms ease-in-out animations
- **Micro-interactions:** Subtle scale and opacity changes on interactive elements
- **Visual Feedback:** Orange highlights and underlines on hover states

### Animation Guidelines
- **Entrance Animations:** Fade-in with slight upward movement (200ms) for major sections
- **Hover Effects:** 
  - Buttons: Scale 1.05 with shadow increase
  - Cards: Lift effect with shadow expansion
  - Links: Orange underline animation from left to right
- **Scroll Animations:** Staggered fade-in for list items and service cards

### Typography System
- **Display Font:** Alexandria Bold/ExtraBold for headlines (h1, h2)
- **Body Font:** Alexandria Regular/Medium for body text and descriptions
- **Accent Font:** Alexandria SemiBold for section titles and CTAs
- **Hierarchy:**
  - H1: 48px, Bold, Black (#111111)
  - H2: 36px, SemiBold, Black (#111111)
  - H3: 24px, Medium, Dark Gray (#3c3c3c)
  - Body: 16px, Regular, Medium Gray (#939598)
  - CTA: 14px, SemiBold, White on Orange background

## Design Sections
1. **Navigation:** Sticky header with dark background, white/orange text
2. **Hero:** Full-width image with overlay, bold headline, CTA button
3. **About:** Two-column layout with text and image, orange accent divider
4. **Services:** Grid of service cards with hover effects
5. **Portfolio:** Masonry-style project showcase
6. **Testimonials:** Carousel of client quotes
7. **Contact:** Form with map integration
8. **Footer:** Dark background with company info and links
